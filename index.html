Abstract

India's judiciary is crucial for maintaining democracy, yet it faces significant challenges, particularly the overwhelming backlog of civil cases. This persistent issue not only delays justice but also undermines public trust in the legal system. This paper examines the Indian court system to identify inefficiencies and propose innovative solutions aimed at expediting justice delivery, focused on civil cases...
